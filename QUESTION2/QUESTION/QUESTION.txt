QUESTION:Explain the difference between Absolute and Relative positioning.
ANSWER:
1) Position Relative :
a) Relative positioning is a positioning method where an element is positioned relative to its normal position.
b) Remains in normal flow.
c) Does not affect sibling elements.
d) Commonly used for fine-tune element positioning.	

2) Position Absolute :
a) Absolute positioning is a positioning method where an element is positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor.
b) Removed from normal flow.
c) Can overlap siblings or be overlapped.
d) Precise placement of floating elements.
